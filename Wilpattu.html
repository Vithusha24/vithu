Introduction of wild life department


Nestled in the Sri Lankan dry zone, Wilpattu National Park reigns as a land of captivating contrasts. Vast open plains dotted with shimmering "willus" (seasonal lakes) give way to verdant patches of dry forest, creating a mosaic of habitats teeming with life. Leopards stalk the grasslands, elephants trumpet serenades from watering holes, and over 200 bird species paint the sky with vibrant hues. Wilpattu, steeped in ancient history and brimming with ecological wonder, invites you to discover the magic of Sri Lanka's wilderness
